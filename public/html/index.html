What is a Table Top game? What is the Fantasy Table Top Compendium?

Table Top games refer to any game that can be played on any flat surface or "table" like the name suggests.

The Fantasy Table Top Compendium is a tool that focuses specifically on roleplaying table top games such as Dungeons & Dragons 5e to Pathfinder. While these games are incredibly rewarding, they also have an incredibly difficult learning curve and isn't as accessible to those just starting out fresh. The Compendium will help you generate character stats with explanations for ability scores, feats, and general character building assistance. This site also provides resources for Game Masters (Also known as Dungeon Masters) to help support encounters, general worldbuilding, and story writing.

Since the basic rules of DnD are provided for free, this site will only cover specific portions of DnD such as poorly explained game mechanics, to even character roleplay and etiquette which is not included in the original rulebook.

Why this tool? Who is this for?

The Table Top Compendium was created as a gateway for creatives to get into table top games without having to endure the steep learning curve that comes with it. TTRPGs are heavily gatekept either through paywalls or a lack of accessible information about getting started. Through the TC Compendium we hope to see more people get involved and really express themselves through the art of character roleplay, worldbuilding, and more importanly getting to have fun through this unique game experience. Whether you are an experienced player or new to the world of TTRPGs there is something here for you~!
