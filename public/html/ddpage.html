Overview

Welcome to the Dungeon Delver's Guide to all things Dungeons & Dragons! You can find all the information you need about getting started with a new character, working with stats, spells, and more! Simply click on any of the links below to get started.

Chapters

Skills, Skill Checks, and Success

Everything you need to know about skills, skill checks, and all things dice related. Learn about how stats and skills go hand in hand, how dice rolls affect the narrative, and maybe learning how to add a little chaos to your campaign through these versatile story choices!


Taking Initiative

Have you ever wanted to employ Guerilla warfare against your adverseries? Perform a literal hit and run? Hide when things get tough? Never fear, the action economy is here to ensure that you have options when in combat or intense scenarios. You'll learn all about Actions, and the different types of options you have when exploring dungeons or taking down Fantasy Capitalism.


Spellcasting 101

Are you tired of hacking and slashing your enemies into submission? Spellcasting is here to save the day! Specializing in both combat and utility, you can never go wrong with flinging magic given the opportunity. Learn about the world of magic, spell components, and creative ways you can bend wording to do your bidding.
