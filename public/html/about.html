Why tabletop games? Why this site?

As an avid lover of acting, drama, and writing, nothing brings me more joy than putting these things together. Dungeons and Dragons has always been something I wanted to drag my other friends into but could not find any easy guides to save my life. It was either behind a 2 hour long video or such a poorly explained guide it would've been better to just buy myself a kit and teach them myself.

That's when I realized I could just upload all of this information myself! So in collaboration with a fellow Dungeon Master, and someone that knows how to use a computer, I embarked on an adventure to provide others an easy to use portal to learn about the world of Dungeons and Dragons.
