Spell Components

Spell Components consist of a number of different things. Think of them like ingredients to a recipe!

There are a wide variety of different spell components for different spells. Be on the look out when you are adventuring.

The last thing you want is to be unable to use a spell because you are missing something. In addition to this, VSM is also very important! VSM stands for vocal, somatic, and material and are parts of your spell. Vocal means that you need to verbally speak something to activate the spell, somatic refers to hand movements or gestures, and material means physical materials you need for the spell.
